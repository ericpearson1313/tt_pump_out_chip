### Forge 1K

Excellent tiny cheap one-time-prgrammable 1K lut device perfect for production of a tiny tapeout scale product until ASIC volumes are reached.

The 3mm x 3mm package is tiny, yet can be soldered by hand.

Its not for the faint of heart though, there is no spare I/O and the gate count is tight. I've found it took a full infrastructure of emulators and monitors to confidently and sucessfully put one of these devices in a system. Really the same level that I would use to bring up a chip.
