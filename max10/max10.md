### Intel/Altera MAX10 

a favourite low cost but capable development fpga. For about $20 gets me a 10M04 with 4K Luts with most-excellent free tools. I chose the 144QFP as I can hand solder it, and there is a high density device (for larger builds) the 10M25 which gives me alot of room.

I use the max10 during development in four roles: System Simulation, Emulation, Monitoring, and its used in production for chip Testing.
